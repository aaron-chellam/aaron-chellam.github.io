Alexander the Great Tribute Page

Alexander the Great was a king of the ancient Greek kingdom of Macedon.

Here are some of the key battles Alexander won against the Persians:

  • The Battle of the Granicus: May 334 BC
  • The Battle of Issus: November 333 BC
  • The Battle of Gaugamela: October 331 BC
  • The Battle of the Persian Gate: January 330 BC
